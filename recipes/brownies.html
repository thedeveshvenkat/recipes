Brownies

This brownie recipe makes rich, fudgy brownies that always turn out well.
They're topped with homemade chocolate frosting to make the best brownies ever!

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cocoa Powder
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  2. Melt 1/2 cup butter in a large saucepan. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar,
    eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, flour, salt, and baking
    powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away
    from the sides of the pan, about 25 to 30 minutes. Let cool briefly before frosting.
  4. To make the chocolate frosting: Combine softened butter, confectioners' sugar,
    3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract in a bowl. Stir until
    smooth. Frost brownies while they are still warm.
